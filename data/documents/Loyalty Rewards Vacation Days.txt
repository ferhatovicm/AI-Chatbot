Loyalty Rewards: Vacation Days

File; LyRewVD_POL_2.00_2023_7_7  

Classification: CONFIDENTIAL

Effective date: February 1, 2020

Note: If you are reading a printed copy of this document, please make sure that there is not a newer version of this document online at Cape Ann Enterprises (Cape Ann) Learning and Development Platform at: (https://lms.capeannenterprises.com/)

A new Cape Ann employee has 20 working days of annual leave during one calendar year in case if one of below requirement is met:

●      The employee spent 6 months in a company for the ongoing year

●      The employee has a long term contract with Cape Ann for more than 6 months.

An employee, before gaining a full-time vacation, is entitled to 1 day of leave for each month of work in Cape Ann.

Employees will get additional days off as a reward for their loyalty.

 

●      After 2 years of consecutive service, employees will get 1 extra day of vacation - 21 days total.

●      After 4 years of consecutive service, employees will get 2 extra days of vacation - 22 days total.

●      After 6 years of consecutive service, employees will get 3 extra days of vacation - 23 days total.

●      After 8 years of consecutive service, employees will get 4 extra days of vacation - 24 days total.

●      After 10 years of consecutive service, employees will get 5 extra days of vacation - 25 days total.

 

Employees’ total number of vacation days will be increased on the day of the anniversary.
